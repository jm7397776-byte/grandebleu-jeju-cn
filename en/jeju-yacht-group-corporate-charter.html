Why it works for corporate & MICE

  • Memorable, not generic. A shared sunset on deck does more for team morale than another hotel ballroom.
  • Catered onboard. Draft beer, wine, tangerine juice, snacks and ramen are included — plus an optional sea-fishing activity to break the ice.
  • Credible & safe. Korea's only certified yacht you can board without a life jacket, with a professional crew and 100,000+ cruises behind it.
  • 5-star hotel partner. Easy to slot into an existing premium itinerary for visiting clients or VIPs.

Great for celebrations, too

It isn't only for business. The same private charter is ideal for:

  • Birthdays and milestone anniversaries
  • Family reunions and multi-generation trips (the stable twin hull suits all ages)
  • Friend groups wanting a private space rather than a shared tour

Because a private charter can flex the route and timing, you can build it around the exact sunset moment and your group's pace.

How to arrange a charter

Charters are best arranged directly so the team can tailor the details. When you get in touch, have these ready:

  • Preferred date (and a backup)
  • Group size
  • The occasion — team building, client hosting, birthday, etc.
  • Any catering, timing or photography requests

The more context you share, the more precisely the crew can plan route, timing and capacity across one or both boats.
